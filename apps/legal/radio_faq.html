Can we play the radio without Internet connection?

Internet connection is required to play the radio stations. We recommend using 3G, 4G or WI FI connection even though radio will play on 2G Connection also.

Can I Play radio in Background?

yes, Press the back button to keep radio playing in the background.

I have problem as Radio App not playing in Background?

Some battery saver apps will close background running applications immediately to prevent battery. Please remove any batery saver application if you have any.

Why Audio stops after some time?

If the radio stations stops after playing for 5 mins only means your battery saver is preventing the app from playing radio in background and closing it .To prevent radio from stopping exclude our app from battery saver apps list..

Why Some stations not playing?

If you are not able to play a station which is Live ( showing as green dots), it is almost your device fault as other people can play those station.

Why some stations are offline?

Some station streaming may not be available so the will not be playing radio station currently offline message will be displayed.

Why some Audio quality is not good?

We don’t have the control over the quality of the audio. We provide the sound quality as streamed by the station.

Can I control Low volume and Equalizer?

Yes but you may install a third party app from play store bass booster, volume booster

Kindly mails us your station details like Station name station logo and stream URL to dsofthelp@outlook.com we will add your station upon validation.

Why my App is Crashing?

Make sure you are on latest app version. Try uninstall and install the app. If still app is still crashing mail us the events when the app is crashing to our email id. We will rectify it in the next update.

How can I updation of stations

You don’t need to update the app to update streaming. The streaming database is updated over internet without updating the App.

Why do we show ads?

We are dependent on ads to continue and run this service. Full page ads will come only when pressing back button.
